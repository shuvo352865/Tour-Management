Places

Bangladesh is a country with many beautiful tourist destinations. Cox's Bazar is famous for having the longest natural sea beach in the world, while Sundarbans is the largest mangrove forest and home to the Bengal tiger. Srimangal is known for its picturesque tea gardens, Dhaka offers a mix of modern and traditional culture, and Rangamati provides breathtaking views of mountains, rivers, and waterfalls, as well as a chance to experience the culture of local ethnic communities. These destinations offer a variety of experiences for tourists, from scenic beauty and adventure to history and culture.
