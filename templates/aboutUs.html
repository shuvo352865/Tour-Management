About our project

We have worked together to develop a web platform for tour management as part of the undergraduate program of SDP 3 course. The project was done under the supervision of Jubayer Al Mahmud Sir. Shahadat has played a significant role in the project, bringing in his technical skills and expertise in programming and web development. He has been responsible for designing and developing the back-end and front-end of the web platform, ensuring that it is user-friendly, efficient, and functional. Supriya has contributed her design skills to the project, designing the user interface of the web platform. She has created a visually appealing and intuitive interface, making it easy for users to navigate and use the platform. Sweety has been the communication lead of the group, responsible for ensuring that all members are on the same page, and communicating with the supervisor about the project's progress. She has also provided valuable feedback and suggestions to improve the platform. Abir has been responsible for the database management of the platform. He has designed and implemented the database schema, ensuring that the data is stored securely and efficiently. He has also been responsible for optimizing the database to improve the performance of the platform. Shakil has been the project manager, responsible for overseeing the entire project and ensuring that it is completed on time and within budget. He has coordinated with the team members, set goals, and prioritized tasks to ensure that the project is delivered successfully. Overall, your group has brought in a diverse range of skills and expertise, allowing you to develop a high-quality web platform for tour management. Your teamwork and collaboration have been instrumental in the success of the project, and your dedication and hard work are commendable.
